The Shake: Tap the cup to rattle the dice. This sets your roll for the round.

The Peek: Press and hold the "PEEK" button to lift the cup. Make sure no one else is looking!

The Play: Use your roll for Liar's Dice or other bluffing games. Blow on the cup to reset and roll again for the next player.
